# TransitOps — Product Scope

TransitOps is a trusted worker-mobility platform for delivery and logistics professionals.

It combines operational evidence with worker-owned progression so companies can source capable
people for higher-responsibility roles, while workers move beyond repetitive, low-paid gigs into
clearer and safer career opportunities.

## The two-sided promise

**For organizations:** post time-bound quality contracts and identify workers through verified
readiness signals: completed contracts, valid licences, safety, qualifications, and relevant cargo
experience.

**For workers:** carry a professional passport between organizations. Delivery volume alone is not
the goal; verified performance unlocks a progression path from local delivery to light cargo,
high-value goods, and truck logistics.

## Fairness rules

- Rankings are readiness signals, not public punishment or a sole hiring decision.
- Reports require evidence and an explicit review outcome; they do not directly reduce XP.
- Organization tier determines review priority, never automatic worker guilt.
- Contracts must be clear, fixed-term, and tier-appropriate rather than one-off exploitative gigs.

## UI principles

- Fast to scan: one primary action and clear status on every screen.
- Consistent: navy/blue foundation, emerald for positive readiness, amber for attention, red only
  for risk or destructive actions.
- Human language: “professional passport”, “verified talent”, and “quality contract” over raw
  operational jargon.
